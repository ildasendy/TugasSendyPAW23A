Me & My Favorit Songs

I probably keep listening to "All Too Well (10 Minute Version)" because it just hits different. The lyrics are super relatable—they capture love, heartbreak, and nostalgia so well that it feels like my own story. Its also an emotional rollercoaster, starting off sweet, then getting painful, and leaving me feeling everything at once. Sometimes, I just need a song that understands me, and this one feels like a warm hug when I'm sad. Plus, the melody is soft but powerful, making it impossible to skip. And maybe, just maybe, it reminds me of someone—a person or a memory that always comes back every time the song plays. Basically, its not just a song—its a feeling.
